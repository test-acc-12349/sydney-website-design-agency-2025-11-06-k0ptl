Cookies and Tracking Technologies

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They allow us to recognize your device and remember your preferences, login information, and browsing history. Cookies help improve your user experience and enable certain website features.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for our website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility features. These cookies cannot be disabled.

Performance Cookies

These cookies collect information about how you use our website, such as which pages you visit and any errors you encounter. This data helps us improve website performance and user experience.

Functional Cookies

These cookies remember your preferences and choices to provide a more personalized experience. They may store language preferences, login information, and customization settings.

Marketing Cookies

These cookies track your behavior across websites to deliver targeted advertisements and measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns. They may be set by third-party advertising partners.

Third-Party Tracking Technologies

We use third-party services that may place their own cookies or tracking pixels on our website, including:

  • Google Analytics: Provides website traffic and user behavior analytics
  • Social Media Pixels: Allow social platforms to track your interactions with our content
  • Advertising Networks: Enable targeted advertising based on your browsing behavior
  • Email Marketing Platforms: Track email open rates and click-through rates

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent. However, blocking cookies may affect website functionality and your user experience. You can also opt out of specific third-party tracking services through their respective opt-out mechanisms.

Some browsers include "Do Not Track" features. Our website currently does not respond to "Do Not Track" signals, but we respect your privacy preferences and provide multiple ways to manage your data collection and use.

Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, and destruction. Our security practices include:

  • SSL/TLS Encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols
  • Secure Servers: Our servers are housed in secure data centers with physical access controls and 24/7 monitoring
  • Access Controls: We limit access to personal information to authorized employees and contractors who need it to perform their duties
  • Regular Audits: We conduct regular security audits and vulnerability assessments to identify and address potential risks
  • Firewalls and Intrusion Detection: We use firewalls and intrusion detection systems to monitor and prevent unauthorized access
  • Data Backups: We maintain regular backups of your data to ensure business continuity and disaster recovery

While we strive to protect your personal information using reasonable security measures, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, and you use our services at your own risk. In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected individuals as required by applicable law.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. The retention period depends on the type of information and the context of our relationship with you:

  • Service Data: Information related to your website project is retained for the duration of our engagement plus 3-5 years for legal and accounting purposes
  • Marketing Communications: Email addresses are retained until you unsubscribe from our mailing list
  • Website Analytics: Aggregated and anonymized data may be retained indefinitely for historical analysis
  • Legal Records: Information may be retained longer if required by law or to establish, exercise, or defend legal claims

You may request deletion of your personal information at any time, subject to legal obligations and legitimate business interests. We will securely delete or anonymize your data upon request, unless we are required to retain it by law.
